About Us

At Kvanttik, our passion lies in pioneering technologies that have the power to transform the world. Currently, we're on a mission to create drones that serve diverse purposes, from revolutionizing agriculture to enhancing medical services and surveillance capabilities. Our dedication to innovation drives us to constantly push the boundaries of what's possible, with the ultimate goal of making these advancements accessible to everyone. With a focus on safety, sustainability, and ethical considerations, we strive to not only improve lives but also contribute positively to society. We believe that by harnessing the power of technology, we can create a better, more sustainable future for all. At Kvanttik, we are committed to empowering the students in schools and colleges with the essential skills and knowledge needed to succeed in today's competitive world. Our training programs are carefully designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world applications, ensuring students are well-prepared for their future careers. Our mission is to inspire and equip students with the tools and confidence to excel in their chosen fields, unlocking their full potential through innovative and comprehensive training.

Our Services

Education

At Kvanttik, we are an EduTech company dedicated to providing hands-on learning in Drone Technology, IoT, Programming, and Industry Readiness. We offer customizable courses, workshops, hackathons, and Campus Recruitment Training (CRT) for schools and colleges. For school students, we focus on Block Programming, making coding easy and interactive, helping them develop a strong foundation in technology. Additionally, we help institutions set up labs and provide long-term mentorship, ensuring students gain practical skills for the future.

Programming For School Students

We believe that coding is an essential skill for the future. Kvanttik provides interactive programming courses for school students. Our hands-on approach makes coding fun and easy to understand, covering topics like Programming, Web Development, IoT and Drone Technologies. Through our sessions, young learners develop problem-solving skills and a strong foundation in programming. All our courses and programs are fully customizable based on the specific requirements of the institution

CRT Trainings

At Kvanttik, we offer specialized Campus Recruitment Training (CRT) programs designed to prepare college and university students for placements. Our training covers aptitude, technical skills, interview preparation, and soft skills, ensuring students are industry-ready. We focus more on practical knowledge, providing hands-on training and real-world applications to help students develop job-ready skills. With expert mentors and a structured curriculum, we help students confidently crack campus placements and secure their dream jobs. All our courses and programs are fully customizable based on the specific requirements of the institution.

Workshops & Hackathons

Kvanttik actively collaborates with schools and colleges to organize workshops and hackathons on drone technology, AI, robotics, and programming. Our events provide students with practical learning experiences, innovation challenges, and hands-on project building. Additionally, if colleges are interested, we help them set up dedicated labs and provide mentorship for students as per their requirement, ensuring they get continuous support and guidance in their technical journey. All our courses and programs are fully customizable based on the specific requirements of the institution.
